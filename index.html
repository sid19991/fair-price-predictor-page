AI Fair Price Predictor

Chrome Extension for Retail & Real Estate Price Valuation

🚀 What it does

This Chrome extension predicts the fair price of retail products (Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, ebay) and even real estate listings (99acres, MagicBricks, Zillow) using AI.

🛒 Retail Products

Predicts fair market price for e-commerce products & shows nearby deals.

🏡 Real Estate

AI-powered valuation reports for flats, villas, and plots.

💳 Monetization Ready

Integrated with Razorpay for subscriptions.

📊 Reports

Export fair price valuation as PDF for sharing or record-keeping.
